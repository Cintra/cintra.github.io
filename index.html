Cintra Consulting Ltd


Our Firm

Cintra Consulting is a management consulting firm whose focus is the planning, justifying, and setting priorities for, capital projects and programs. We help public, private and non-profit sector clients assess their short and long term infrastructure needs. We help turn such needs into options, then solutions, and finally reality.

Details of the federal government $14 Billion New Building Canada Fund, a key component of the $53 Billion New Building Canada Plan, have been recently announced: New Building Canada Fund. Proposals under the new Plan were being accepted until March 31, 2014. This deadline has passed, but the story is not over once the government investment decision is made.



What can Cintra do for you?


Are you a public sector client who will receive funding under the New Building Canada Fund or another provincial or federal program? Are you required to provide supporting documentation to senior government agencies before this funding will be committed, during project implementation and post completion?

Cintra Consulting is ready to provide expertise in guiding development of or completing a government supplied business case template or related document for this new funding program.

Are you a private company representative facing difficult capital investment decisions? Do you know what is needed, but could use a fresh set of eyes on it and require the supporting material to convince management or the Board of Directors?

Cintra Consulting can provide and document a rationale approach to investment decisions.

We offer the following:

  • Capital program development
  • Capital project development
  • Federal and Provincial business case document review and preparation
  • Project Definition Reports
  • Project monitoring and update reports
  • Public and stakeholder consultation
  • Consultant and contractor oversight
  • Project Close Out reports
  • Non-profit grant funding applications
Are you a non-profit representative contributing to infrastructure investment decisions? Are you looking for a voice of experience to build a solid case?

“Non-profits have played a vital role in building communities in Canada and continue to make important contributions to developing public infrastructure.” (from BUILDING STRONGER COMMUNITIES: NON-PROFIT SECTOR PARTICIPATION IN INFRASTRUCTURE PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT) Cintra Consulting can be in the background asking the relevant questions, or can be sitting with you at the table and defending your position.



Who We Are


Lisa Brown, P.Eng. M.Sc, has been active in project development for the past 10 years. She has been supporting clients in developing projects, applying for government support, providing client oversight, and monitoring of funded projects. She has held numerous business management, project management and project development roles for large governmental organizations and construction firms. Prior to her experience in project development she held engineering roles in international, multidisciplinary engineering and design firms.

Lisa holds a bachelor's and master's degree in engineering from the University of British Columbia and the University of Wisconsin – Madison, respectively. She has lived and worked in Canada, the USA, Germany and England.
